Notice

Thank you so much for your support and for choosing to download the "full version" of the mod.

Fortunately, I have eventually decided to discontinue this model, as I felt was too much of an inconvenience to people. It made people jump through hoops by downloading the mod from elsewhere and then re-installing it, and it also made it harder to keep track of updates, as people would have to manually download each update the same way, and so on..

Right now, all you have to do is go back to your launcher (or wherever else you installed the mod), and update the mod from the same place you downloaded it from (aka CurseForge or Modrinth). If you want you can still visit my website where I also publish the mod, but that is up to you.
